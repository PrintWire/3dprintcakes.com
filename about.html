About 3D Print Cakes

Custom edible 3D printed desserts and confections

OUR STORY

Who We Are

Traditional cake decoration is limited by what a human hand can consistently execute at scale — and by the economics of custom sugar work, which prices most events out of truly bespoke confectionery. 3D Print Cakes breaks that constraint. Using food-safe additive deposition, we achieve filigree detail, parametric geometry, and portrait-level relief that no pastry chef can replicate by hand.

Founded by the team behind ChiLab Studio — digital fabrication specialists with SAIC faculty credentials and project credits including the Uber HQ feature wall — we applied the same precision-first philosophy to edible materials. The result: confectionery that meets the quality bar of high-end architectural fabrication, delivered for weddings, luxury events, and branded experiences.

Our Mission

To make precision edible printing accessible for any occasion — proving that bespoke confectionery doesn't have to be a luxury reserved for the few. Every wedding topper, every corporate bonbon, every sugar sculpture printed to the same exacting standard.

Our Vision

A world where every edible form — however complex, however personalized — can be produced by digital deposition. Where the pastry kitchen and the parametric design studio are the same room.

OUR VALUES

What Drives Us

🍳

Food Safety First

Every material we deposit is 100% food-safe, produced in an allergen-aware facility. No resins, no plastics — only edible-grade substrates certified for human consumption.

◆

Precision

0.4mm minimum feature resolution. Pantone-matched color targets. Dimensional accuracy you can verify against your brief — because a wedding topper that's slightly off is unacceptable.

♥

Craft

Digital fabrication and artisan confectionery are not opposites. Every printed piece is inspected and hand-finished by our team before it leaves the studio.

☕

Occasion Intelligence

We understand that a wedding topper, a corporate bonbon, and a birthday sculpture are not the same problem. We tailor material, scale, and lead time to what the occasion actually demands.
