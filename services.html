Our Services

Edible 3D printing for weddings, events, and luxury confectionery — sugar sculptures, chocolate toppers, and fondant details no pastry chef can hand-craft

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Expertise

Custom Toppers & Figurines

Wedding monograms, portrait reliefs, company logos, and bespoke figurines precision-printed in food-safe isomalt sugar or tempered white chocolate. Edible colorant throughout — no surface paint — ensuring long shelf life and safe consumption by guests of any age.

Sugar Sculpture Printing

Freestanding architectural centerpieces, geometric statement pieces, and figurative sugar sculptures at 1:1 scale or larger. Clear isomalt achieves a sugar-glass translucency that no mold can replicate. Hollow construction keeps finished pieces lightweight and structurally stable on the table.

Chocolate Fine Detail

Tempered Belgian couverture deposited in intricate filigree lattice, relief panels, and showpiece elements at 0.4mm minimum feature. Available in dark, milk, and white chocolate. Bonbon shell printing and custom bar mold masters are standard offerings — gift-ready packaging included.

Fondant & Royal Icing Systems

Multi-layer fondant decorations with precision-cut geometry and Pantone-matched color targets. Royal icing structural elements bridge fine detail work between printed components. Wafer-paper printing enables ultra-thin photographic transfers applied directly to fondant surfaces — perfect for corporate branding and portrait cakes.

OUR PROCESS

How We Work

1

Design Brief

Share your concept via sketch, photo reference, or 3D file. We review occasion details, size requirements, color targets, and dietary restrictions — including nut, gluten, and dairy constraints — before quoting.

2

Material & Color Specification

We select the right food-safe substrate — isomalt sugar, tempered chocolate, fondant, or royal icing — for your design. Colors are matched to Pantone references or your provided samples. A digital proof is sent for approval before printing begins.

3

Precision Print & Artisan Finish

Parts are printed at 0.4mm resolution and inspected before any hand-finishing work. Detail color, structural joins, and surface polish are applied by our confectionery team. Each piece is photographed for your records.

4

Packaging & Delivery

Finished pieces ship in temperature-controlled, humidity-buffered packaging designed for edible goods. Local Chicago-area orders can be hand-delivered. Nationwide shipping available with a 3-day lead time before your event date.
